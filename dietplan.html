Fitness trackers are wearable devices that monitor and track various aspects of your physical activity, health, and wellness. They can track:

  • Steps taken: Daily step count and progress toward goals
  • Distance traveled: Distance walked, run, or cycled
  • Calories burned: Estimated energy expenditure
  • Heart rate: Continuous or periodic heart rate monitoring
  • Sleep patterns: Sleep quality, duration, and stages
  • Exercise tracking: Specific workout tracking, such as running, cycling, or strength training
Fitness trackers can help you:
  • Set and achieve fitness goals
  • Monitor progress
  • Stay motivated
  • Improve overall health and wellness
